Animal Farm and the Russian Revolution

Animal Farm by George Orwell there are multiple correlations between the plot in the book and the events of the Russian revolution to World War Two. On this website you will see a display of Characters and who has portrayed them in real life indirectly. Judging by all the research done by our group we can tell that either directly or indirectly this book was influenced by current events at the time of the writing of the book.
