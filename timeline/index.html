Timeline

1914

  • World War I begins

    The countries of Europe are plunged into war and as a result chaos. World order is challenged, much like the challenges to order preseted in Orwell's novel.

    July 15 (July 28 Gregorian)

1915

  • Czar Nicholas II assumes command of the Russian Army

    Nicholas II takes autocratic measures much like Farmer Jones frantically tries to keep his farm under control.

    September 5 (September 18 Gregorian)

1917

  • The February Revolution

    Called March Revolution on the Gregorian calendar, it begans with strikes and demonstrations in Petrograd, represented in the novel by Old Major starting to speak about revolution.

    February 23-27 (March 8-12 Gregorian)

  • Czar Nicholas II Abdicates

    The following day, Nicholas' brother, Mikhail announced his refusal to accept the throne and the Provisional Government was formed. In Animal Farm, Farmer Jones takes measures to try to save his farm, like decorating Molly to prove that he still cares about the animals.

    March 2 (March 15 Gregorian)

  • The October Revolution - the Bolsheviks take over Petrograd (called the November Revolutionon the Gregorian calendar), In Animal Farm, this is depicted by the Battle of the Cowshed.

    October 25 (November 7 Gregorian)

1918

  • The Formation of the Communist Party

    The Bolshevik Party changes its name and Communism becomes the main ideology of Russia, much the same way that Animalism is the ideology of the Farm.

    March 8

  • Russian Civil War Begins

    June

  • Nicholas II and his Family are Executed

    The novel is less violent and Farmer Jones is forced off his farm in a coup.

    July 17

1920

  • Russian Civil War Ends

    November

1922

  • The Union of Soviet Socialist Republics (U.S.S.R.) established

    In the novel, they rename the farm, Animal Farm.

    December 30

1924

  • Lenin Dies

    Stalin will become his successor, and Napoleon becomes the sucessor to Old Major.

    January 21
